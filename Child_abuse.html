Project information

  • Name: Child Abuse Analysis
  • Category: Data Analysis
  • Tool: Power BI, SQL, Excel
  • Project date: 18 May, 2025
  • Project URL: GitHub

This interactive and dynamic dashboard transforms raw child abuse data into a compelling narrative for social awareness and policy-making. Key findings include:

  • 401K Parents, 518K Perpetrators, 1,791 Child Day Care Providers, and 1,616 Legal Guardians analyzed.
  • Top states like Texas, New York, and Illinois lead in child care providers.
  • California and New York report the highest perpetrator rates, with 210K cases.
  • 47% of cases concentrated in six states, highlighting the need for targeted prevention.

This dashboard is not just a visualization—it’s a call to action for reform, empathy, and impact, showcasing how modern analytics can drive awareness and change.
